One Day

Dear Cassia,

Words can not explain how much you mean to me. The way you make me feel, the way you look at me, the way you smile. Never in my wildest dreams would I imagine me making this, let alone having ever talking to you again, especially on this level.

I know i've said that I had completely forgotton you before, left you in the past, never found any means to talk to you. It was better for me to leave you in the past as a coping mechanism and for me to find peace.But on the rare occassions that I would see you these past few years, there was this inexplicable feeling I got whenever i saw you. a feeling of seeing someone, especially your first love, someone who you shared everything with. Secrets, plans, how each others days went.

It was crazy to think that someone like that had become a complete stranger. but one of those ones where you still had love for at the end of the day and wished nothing but the best because that's all you could really do

And I thought it would be the end of that. just a foggy memory of the past...

But somehow, someway, our paths crossed again, and I guess we have my dad to thank because of that. You hitting me up, coming to visit, I thought nothing of it, just you showing your respects. I know what was going through your head, maybe it was the same thoughts of mine. nothing but showing your support. But in a way, things worked out the way they did, with us and the group going for a walk, points where it was just me and you talking, I guess we were long overdue having a conversation together

I remember it. When we were asked "why did you guys break up before?", and us not really having a straight answer, resurfaced so many memories in that instant about our past together. Mind you, I still thought you were in a relationship when this all went down, so it did feel kind of weird talking about our past like that. Me telling you about how you were the reason I started working out, you being kinda salty for not hitting you up on your birthday. I guess it was the perfect segway into us exchanging numbers

Thoughts of what if you didn't hit me up that one morning, asking me if I was up, how different would our lives be at this moment? I never thought much of our first text conversation to be honest with you. Thought it was just us catching up on each others lives. There was definitely a wall over me, protecting me from expecting what was going to come about talking to you again after all these years. But like I said before, it's like you reached out to me, back turned on you walking away from the past, grabbing my arm to get your attention. that moment when you messaged me, wondering if I was up, wishing i was with you at the hospital, was in a way you telling me "let's go, let's do this again, let's do this right"

And from there, it's all history, a story we'll tell to anybody who has an ear to listen. Facetiming all night till the sun came up just like how we were when we were younger. Seeing each other and going on what was clearly dates but couldn't really label it as that. I did however acknowledge that some the shit we were doing was lowkey romantic af

I'd be lying if i said i wasn't afraid of what would come about us rekindling our relationship. Me wanting to take things slow to make sure we were both doing the right thing. But it didn't take long for me to realize that I did want you. I realized how much I had missed you after all that time we spent apart.

I guess we did take things slow in the sense of not talking to each other for 6 years doing our own things, experiencing and learning important life lessons that would inevitably draw us back to one another as two completely grown up individuals. That night when we kissed for the first time, how you first squeezed me so tightly, made me realize how much you wanted me and missed me. Made me realize that you were the one i wanted to call mine again

But after all of that I was still scared, remembering the past and fearing if history would repeat itself held me back. Getting advice from the outside just made me more confused, but once i set the outside noise aside and listened to what my heart was telling me, I knew I wanted you.

And I haven't looked back since

This past month has been amazing to say the least. the things we've done, the places we been to together, just being able to make all of these memories together with someone as special as you, has made me the happiest I've been in so long

It gives me a sense of security knowing you'd do as much for me as I would for you, even more. You continue to amaze me with all of the things you do not just for me, but the people that you love. You constantly go out of your way to make sure the ones in your circle are good and that's just one of the things I love about you. The fact that you would even drive all the way to Oshawa to just see me makes me know you're for real. Even going on our cottage trip with everyone, sharing a room with you, being comfortable as a couple in front of others.

It just gives me pride to be able to call you all mine. Like how could somebody not want you? I guess we had to take that time apart to realize how much we missed and needed each other. I want to be the one you need to lie their shoulder on. I want to hear whatever you want to say, I'm all ears. I want to be there to celebrate with you when you're happy as well as when you're at your lowest. I don't want you to ever go through anything alone ever again.

You deserve the world and more.

I know it is still earlier in our rekindled relationship, but with our history and the lessons we learned from these past few years, I can guarantee you I will always love you unconditionally. I can't imagine us ever arguing or fighting because I really am over the petty shit. I know you are too.

I just want to grow with you. That's what a relationship is really all about, bringing each other up and supporting one another in whatever they do. With you, I can honestly say I can do anything. There's so many things I want to do and I'm so happy that you're the one I'm going to be making all these memories with. I want you to be by my side through it all

I'm so lucky to call you mine, again. It all still feels like a dream. I could never imagine us looking at each other the way we first did back then. Seeing me put a smile on your face, being able to hold you and kiss you, sharing the same bed with you, it all just feels right. It's all I ever wanted with you before. All of these memories of the past of us talking about how we are now just makes me think to myself that it's been a long time coming baby.

Thinking of it now we are living in that one day we used to talk about and now, this 'one day' has turned into having a future together, living together, it's all so crazy to still think about.

I feel like if our past selves, the ones that were still together, constantly talking about the one day we would be doing the things we're doing together right now, going out in public, not being afraid of people seeing us, going on adventures together, spending time at each others places, would make them incredibly proud.

Not many people are lucky enough to have a story like ours. It's definitely a unique one but a great one to tell. Usually people breakup and just leave it at that but I will always think about how incredible our story of how we got back together is

I know this is a lot to read but I promise next time it won't be so long. It's just that we've gone through a lot by ourselves these last 6 years and this being the first time i get to make something like this for you I wanted to make it special because you deserve it.

I want you to know now and forever you will never be alone Cassia. We've both had our fun during the time we spent apart but now that we're back together I'm ready for us. Everyday is a celebration knowing I got you. And you will always have me, I'm not going anywhere and I'm going to make sure I will keep you happy. There's nothing I want more to be the source of your joy as well as the one who wipes the tears off your face when you're down.

I want to be the one to hold you down no matter the situation. I'm prepared for whatever comes our way. And if we ever have days where we aren't in the best moods I'll make it an effort to ensure you that it's always love at the end of the day.
